Visual Design Portfolio

Piece 1: Mandala Project: I made this project with the help of my teacher and some of my classmates. In the process of completing this project, I learned how to use different tools in adobe photoshop. For example, I used the rotate tool and the paint bucket tool in order to make this project the way I wanted it to be. For my base, I used a flower and I chose the colors red and light blue. When I duplicated my layers 5 times, I noticed the shadows come together and the shape coming together in a spiral form. Piece 2: Pixel Character Project, For this project, I use the pencil tool and I thought it would be a good idea to make a grim reaper for my pixel art character. I started by making the outline of the grim reaper’s character model and proceeding to color in the inside of said character with the colors black, white and some brown and some gray. My character is wearing an all black outfit since grim reapers tend to wear black cloaks and my character is holding a scythe. I think my character would be in an action game where its purpose would be to greet people at death’s door since the grim reaper is the personification of death. Piece 3: What's Your Vibe Project, I used the program adobe express to create a collage based off of my 6 word memoir. I started by creating that memoir and I chose to make that memoir, “Always work for what you want.” When finding the pictures needed for my memoir, I specifically searched up pictures/paintings with the theme of hardwork and/or success. I included these pictures because they all correlate with each other since in order to be successful, you have to put in effort and work for that success you crave so much. I believe my collage doesn’t match my aesthetic because I just found pictures and paintings that didn’t have any similar appearance. If I were to re-do this collage I would honestly end up making my own drawings and create my own aesthetic off of that.
